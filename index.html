About Me

Hi! I’m Talia.
I received my degree at the Art Institute of Charlotte, I’m a passionate graphic designer looking to become a member of an innovative and productive design team. I’m looking to find a place where quality, collaboration and creativity co-exist.
I have 8 years of Environmental Graphic Design experience. I create concepts, develop proofs and layout print-ready files for clients with existing brands. I have experience managing production and fabrication teams, within tight timelines, in order to meet our client’s needs. Through my environmental design work, I have also attained meaningful experience with the following: Visual identity design, Marketing and Advertising design, Publication design, Presentation Design and Art and Illustration design.
